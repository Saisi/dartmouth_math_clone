Quick Question

Could this graph model the number of rabbits in a pen of fixed size and fixed daily allotment of food over a ten-year period?

Answer

Possibly, but assuming the rabbits are healthy, the pen would have to be very large and the food allotment very great in comparison with the number of rabbits. Otherwise, overcrowding or food shortages would cause the population to level off.
