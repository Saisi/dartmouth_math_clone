Examples

  • Let f(x) = x2 and g(x) = x. Find (f + g)'(4). Does this equal f '(4) + g'(4)?
  • Draw the derivative of a given graph.
  • Let g(x) = 1/x. Find g'(x), g''(x), and g'''(x), and graph them. Can you find a formula for the nth derivative of g(x)?
