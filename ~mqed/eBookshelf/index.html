Welcome to the electronic bookshelf of materials that feature an interdisciplinary approach to a mathematical topic. Some of these materials may be downloaded directly and others may be obtained by contacting the listed source. Many were developed as part of the Math Across the Curriculum Project at Dartmouth College. This work was supported in part by the National Science Foundation.
