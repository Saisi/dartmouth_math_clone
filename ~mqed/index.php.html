Our Mission

The Center for Mathematics and Quantitative Education supports the teaching and learning of quantitative subjects across a variety of contexts. Collaborating with national and international projects, the Center provides resources that address a range of pedagogical concerns.

Our Areas of Expertise

Publishing

The Center publishes interdisciplinary materials in quantitative areas suitable for elementary to post-secondary students. These are available online via The Electronic Bookshelf (Secondary and Post Secondary) and the The Little Bookshelf in the Big Woods (Elementary and Secondary).

Evaluation

The Center has considerable experience conducting formative and summative evaluation of courses, curriculum and projects.

Professional Development

The Center has collaborated with institutions nationwide on faculty development workshops across a range of content and levels.

Curriculum Development

The Center has participated in and promoted a variety of curriculum development projects including Money Matters and Math Across the Curriculum

MQED is part of The National Numeracy Network and is partially funded by The National Science Foundation, The Woodrow Wilson National Fellowship Foundation, and Dartmouth College.
