Bilipschitz equivalence is not equivalent to quasi-isometric
equivalence for finitely generated groups

Tullia Dymarz

Yale University


We show that certain lamplighter groups that are
quasi-isometric (even commensurable) are not bilipschitz equivalent.
The proof involves structure of quasi-isometries from rigidity
theorems, analysis of bilipschitz maps of the n-adics and uniformly
finite homology.
