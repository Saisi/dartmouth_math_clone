Positively curved manifolds with large conjugate radius

Ben Schmidt

Michigan State University


I'll describe a proof that simply connected Riemannian manifolds with sectional curvatures at least one and conjugate radius at least pi/2 have equal injectivity and conjugate radii. Using this result, I'll deduce some metric characterizations of compact rank one symmetric spaces, including a generalization of Berger's classical rigidity theorem for simply connected manifolds with positive quarter pinched sectional curvatures.
