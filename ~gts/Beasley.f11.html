Supersymmetric Chern-Simons theory on contact manifolds

Chris Beasley

Northeastern University


Introduced by Witten in 1989, Chern-Simons gauge theory
serves as the premier example of a topological quantum field theory
(TQFT). In this talk, based upon joint work with Jonathan Weitsman, I
will discuss a new class of three-dimensional TQFTs which derive from
the supersymmetric extension of Chern-Simons theory. Among their
interesting features, the new TQFTs involve both contact geometry and
D-branes in an essential way.
