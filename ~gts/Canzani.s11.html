Scalar curvature of random metrics

Yaiza Canzani

McGill University


We study Gauss curvature for random Riemannian metrics on a compact surface, lying in a fixed conformal class; our questions are motivated by comparison geometry. We will explain how to estimate the probability that Gauss curvature will change sign after a random conformal perturbation of the metric. We will also discuss some extremal problems for that probability and their relation to other extremal problems in spectral geometry.

This is joint work with D.Jakobson and I.Wigman.
