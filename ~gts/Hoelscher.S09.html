Low dimensional cohomogeneity one manifolds

Corey Hoelscher

Rutgers University


A cohomogeneity one manifold is a Riemannian manifold M with lots of symmetry, in the sense that any point in M can be carried to some point on a fixed geodesic in M by some isometry of M. We will start by describing the classification of compact simply connected cohomogeneity one manifolds in dimensions 7 and lower. We will then discuss recent progress toward understanding the topology of these manifolds.
