Topology and Combinatorics

John McCleary

Vassar College


Topology studies spaces that include spaces of all possible configurations of
combinatorial problems. Often the configurations come with symmetry and the
problem at hand can be rewritten as a linear condition on a test map. Within
this framework, topological methods can be made to give concrete combinatorial
results. In joint work with Pavle and Alexandra Blagojevic, we use algebraic
topology to carry out this method.
