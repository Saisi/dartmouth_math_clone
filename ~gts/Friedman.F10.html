Additivity and non-additivity of perverse singularities

Greg Friedman

Texas Christian University


The Novikov Additivity and Wall Non-additivity theorems relate the signature of a manifold, respectively a manifold with boundary, to the signatures of the pieces when the manifold is cut in two. I will discuss joint work with Eugenie Hunsicker in which we extend these results to a signature of stratified spaces that is defined using intersection homology.
