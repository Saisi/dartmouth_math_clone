A few remarks on Lagrangian cobordisms

Roman Golovko (Universite de Montreal)


We will discuss some obstructions to the existence of exact Lagrangian cobordisms
in the symplectization of the standard contact space and some constructions of Lagrangian cobordisms. In addition, we will construct infinitely many pairs of Lagrangian cobordant and not pairwise Legendrian isotopic Legendrian n-tori in R^{2n+1}.
