Conformal maps from spacetimes to the space of Legendrian submanifolds and cuasality on causally simple spacetimes

Vladimir Chernov

Dartmouth College


We discuss a few constructions related to our joint work with Stefan Nemirovski. In particular we discuss the conformal embedding of a spacetime into the space of Legendrian manifolds. We also discuss when one may hope that the link formed by spheres of light rays through two points x,y of a spacetime determines causal relation between x and y.
