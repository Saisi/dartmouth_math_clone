Invariants for Legendrian Links from Generating Families

Lisa Traynor

Bryn Mawr


Legendrian knots are topological knots that satisfy an additional geometric condition imposed by a contact structure. Graphs of real-valued functions can be "lifted" from the plane to 3-space in a unique way to become a Legendrian curve. In this way, one can think of Legendrian curves as being "generated" by functions. I will describe how this procedure can be extended to generate more complicated curves, and how one can use these functions to define invariants of Legendrian links.
