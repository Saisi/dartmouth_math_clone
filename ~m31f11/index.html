Prerequisites:

Math 22 or 24, Linear Algebra, is a prerequisite for Math 31.

If you are planning to take Math 81 in the future, I strongly advise you to take Math 71 instead of Math 31. I am happy to meet with you individually to discuss whether Math 71 or Math 31 is more appropriate for you.
