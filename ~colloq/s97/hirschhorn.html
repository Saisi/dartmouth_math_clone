speaks on

Localizing spaces and model categories


Abstract. We describe how algebraically localizing homotopy and homology groups of spaces led to "localizing" the spaces themselves. This is best understood as a localization of the homotopy category, but for this we must be careful about what we mean by "the homotopy category". The best way to make this clear is by noticing that we're really doing homotopy theory in a "model category", and that we're "localizing" the model category structure.
