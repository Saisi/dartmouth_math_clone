speaks on

Manifolds with positive and nonnegative sectional curvature



Abstract. An important class of manifolds in Riemannian geometry are those with positive curvature (or more generally nonnegative curvature). In spite of much work on this subject, very few examples are known, all of which are closely related to Lie groups and Lie group actions. We will discuss known examples among homogeneous spaces, biquotients of Lie groups, and cohomogeneity one manifolds.
