Professor Monika Nitsche


Ohio State University


speaks on

Vortex Sheet motion and the formation of Vortex Rings


Abstract.A vortex sheet is a mathematical model for a shear layer in which the finitely thick layer is approximated by a surface. Ideally, the solution to the model equations approximates the solution to the real viscous flow equations in the limit of zero viscosity. I will first present the numerical issues that arise in solving the model vortex sheet equations, and then study applications to simulate a real viscous flow, the formation of a vortex ring.
