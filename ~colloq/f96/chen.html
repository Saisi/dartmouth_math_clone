Professor Jingyi Chen


Massachussetts Institute of Technology


speaks on

Minimal surfaces in symplectic 4-manifolds


Abstract.An adjunction number is defined for each surface in a Riemannian 4-manifold. For minimal surfaces in symplectic 4-manifolds, the adjunction numbers can be used to study pseudoholomorphic curves, totally real minimal surfaces and Lagrangian minimal surfaces.
