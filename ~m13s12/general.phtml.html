Homework Policy

  • Webwork problems will be assigned daily. They will be due at 8:45am of the next class day.
  • Written assignments will be assigned weekly. They will be due each Monday in class, and they will typically cover the material up through the previous Wednesday.
  • Late homework will not be accepted. If you feel there is a very legitimate reason for your homework to be late you should discuss this with your instructor in advance. Unexcused late or missing papers count zero.


Grades

The course grade will be based upon the exams and the homework as follows:

Webwork 10%
Written homework 10%
Exam 1 25%
Exam 2 25%
Final Exam 30%


The Honor Principle

On Exams: No help given or received. Exams are closed book and that no calculators are permitted.

On Homework: You are permitted and encouraged to collaborate with others on the homework. However, after discussing the problems, you should each write up the final solutions in your own words. It is a violation of the honor code for someone to provide the answers for you.
