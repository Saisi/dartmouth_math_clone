Abstract: Let K be function field k(t) and Kd=k(t1/d) where k is a finite field and d is prime to p=char(k). Let E be the Legendre elliptic curve y2=x(x+1)(x+t) over K. It turns out that one can compute the rank of E(Kd) for all d and also prove that the Tate-Shafarevich group Ш(E/Kd) is finite for all d. For some d one may even compute the group structure of Ш(E/Kd). These computations are in terms of invariants of the subgroup of (Z/dZ)* generated by p and are of a somewhat combinatorial flavor. Analyzing the formulas (e.g., determining the set of d for which the rank is non-zero or determining average behavior as d varies) looks challenging. I'll explain how to compute the rank and the structure of Ш and pose several problems.
