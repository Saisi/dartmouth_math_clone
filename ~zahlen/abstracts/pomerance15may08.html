Abstract: In a seminar I gave in winter term I mentioned a theorem that describes those n for which the only group of order n is cyclic; the condition is that n and phi(n) are coprime. I also mentioned the theorem of Erdos that gives an asymptotic formula for the distribution of such numbers n. In this talk I'll discuss the proof of the Erdos theorem, plus some recent extensions. The Erdos proof will get us into some of the standard tools of elementary--analytic number theory, including Brun's sieve method.
