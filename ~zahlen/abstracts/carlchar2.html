Brizolis has asked if for every prime p>3 there is a primitive root g for p and an integer a in the range 1 to p-1 such that g^{a} and a are congruent mod p. Put another way, the residue a might be thought of as a fixed point for the discrete log function with base g. In this talk I will use the Polya--Vinogradov inequality to prove the Brizolis conjecture for all sufficiently large primes p.
