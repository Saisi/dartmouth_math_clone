I am a visiting professor in the mathematics department at Dartmouth College, studying algebraic number theory. Previously, I was a graduate student at Stanford University, working with Akshay Venkatesh. My research focuses on the mathematics surrounding the inverse Galois problem and number fields with limited ramification.
