Complex Analysis:

Chapters I-IV and VII §1-4 of Conway's (John B.) book Complex Analysis I is a pretty complete treatment. Brown & Churchill's Complex Variables and Applications is a standard treatment, but lacks sophistication. Chapter 10 of Rudin's Real & Complex Analysis is a very terse and sophisticated supplemental source.
