Mathematics Teaching Seminar

Application

Applicants must have completed at least one year of graduate study in mathematics, and be currently enrolled in a mathematics graduate program. We will consider applicants who show potential to become excellent teachers of mathematics. Accepted students should be prepared to do a significant preparatory reading and writing assignment before the seminar begins, and to arrange for a faculty member to observe their teaching (either in their own class, or as a guest teacher) after they return to their program.

Applications for the 2012 Teaching Seminar will be accepted through June 5, 2012. Tuition is $5,565. Tuition assistance may be available.
