About Me

I am a 4th year graduate student in the Department of Mathematics at Dartmouth College. My advisor is Sergi Elizalde. My research interests are in enumerative combinatorics with an emphasis on bijective techniques. Specifically, I have worked on problems in pattern avoidance, set partitions, matchings, and posets.
