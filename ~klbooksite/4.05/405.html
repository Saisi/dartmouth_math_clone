4.5 Techniques of Integration

 

Summary

Two techniques of integration are discussed, specifically, the Method of Substitution and Integration by Parts.

By the end of your studying, you should know:

  • How to use differentials (introduced in 3.1).
  • The method of substitution for indefinite and definite integrals.
  • How to apply the integration by parts formula to indefinite and definite integrals.
