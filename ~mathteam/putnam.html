PUTNAM TRAINING

Thousands of students from all across the United States write the Putnam Exam every year, because it's a wonderful way to express problem solving ability and have some fun doing mathematics.

The Mathematics Department trains students interested in the Putnam (or just problem solving in general) every Fall Term.

This year, the Putnam Training Sessions are hosted by Professors Sergi Elizalde and Andrew Yang, so stop on by whether you're interested in mathematical problem solving, just curious, or want to relax and enjoy some Ramunto's Pizza with us!
