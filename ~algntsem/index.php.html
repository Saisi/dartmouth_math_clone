Algebra and Number Theory Seminar
This is an informal seminar, currently meeting Tuesdays, from 1 -- 3 pm in 201 Kemeny.

It is intended more as a reading seminar in algebraic number theory than a research seminar in that many of the talks present and discuss the results of papers we have been meaning to read, but have never quite gotten around to.
