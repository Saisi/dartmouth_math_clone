Homework Assigments

IMPORTANT NOTE:

You may get no credit for your homework unless it is COMPLETELY LEGIBLE, on standard letter-sized paper, stapled, and identified with your name.

Homework assigned each day will be collected on the following Monday (or Wednesday, if class does not meet on Monday). It is, however, a good idea to do each day's homework before the next class.

Reading assigned each day should be done by the next class. So, for example, Section 1.3, listed on Monday January 10, is assigned on Monday and should be read by Wednesday, January 12. The same applies to discussion questions and review.

The final paper assignment includes the due dates for preliminary problems, first draft, and final version.
