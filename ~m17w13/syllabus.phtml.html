Tentative syllabus

Here is a rough list of the topics that I plan to cover by week. A more detailed description will be given in the Homework Assignments page as the course progresses.

  • Week 1. Fibonacci numbers
  • Week 2. Compositions, binomial coefficients, subsets
  • Week 3. Lattice paths, multisets
  • Week 4. Permutations, derangements, inclusion-exclusion
  • Week 5. Involutions, cycles, Stirling numbers
  • Week 6. More permutation statistics, alternating permutations, integer partitions
  • Week 7. Partition bijections, Harmonic numbers and Stirling numbers
  • Week 8. Parking functions, Catalan numbers
  • Week 9. Student presentations
