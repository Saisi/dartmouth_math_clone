Tentative syllabus

Here is a tentative list of topics that will be convered, together with the corresponding references.

  • Basic combinatorics.

    • Catalan numbers. Sets and multisets. Compositions.
      [dM] Chapter 1.
      Other references:    [Aig] Sections 1.1-1.2.    [EC1] Section 1.2.

    • Integer and set partitions. Stirling numbers. Permutations.
      [dM] Chapter 3.
      Other references:    [Aig] Sections 1.3-1.5.    [vLW] Chapter 13.    [EC1] Section 1.3.

    • Inclusion-Exclusion.
      [dM] Chapter 2.
      Other references:    [Aig] Section 5.1.    [EC1] Sections 2.1-2.3.    [vLW] Chapter 10.

  • Generating functions.

    • Recurrences. Formal power series.
      [dM] Chapter 4.
      Other references:    [Aig] Sections 2.1, 2.2, 3.1.    [Wf] Section 2.1.    [vLW] Chapter 14.

    • The symbolic method. Unlabeled structures. Ordinary generating functions.
      [dM] Chapter 5.
      Other references:    [FS] Chapter 1.    [Wf] Section 2.2.

    • Labeled structures. Exponential generating functions.
      [dM] Chapter 6.
      Other references:    [Aig] Section 3.3.    [FS] Chapter 2.    [Wf] Section 2.3.

  • Topics in algebraic combinatorics.

    • Partially ordered sets. Chains and antichains. Sperner's theorem.
      [St] Chapter 4.

    • Young tableaux. The RSK algorithm.
      [St] Chapter 8.
      Other references:    [EC2] Section 7.11.    [BS] Section 4.

    • Group actions on boolean algebras.
      [St] Chapter 5.

    • Young diagrams and q-binomial coefficients.
      [St] Chapter 6.
      Other references:    [Aig] Section 1.6.

    • Enumeration under group action. Counting orbits using the Burnside Lemma. Polya's theorem.
      [St] Chapter 7.
      Other references:    [Aig] Sections 6.1-6.3.
