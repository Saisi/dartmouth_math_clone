Zebediah Engberg

I am a fifth-year graduate student in the Department of Mathematics at Dartmouth College. I graduated from Hampshire College in 2009 and received a masters degree from Dartmouth College in 2011. My advisor is Carl Pomerance, and my research interests are in elementary analytic number theory and anatomy of integers.
