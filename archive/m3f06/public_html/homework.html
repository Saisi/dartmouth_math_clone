Homework assignments

The written homework assignment is to be handed in weekly in class on Friday. WebWork assignments are due by 5pm on the day of the next class. (exceptions will be noted on the assignment schedule below)

Effective immediately, every homework handed in must include the following honor statement

All the work on this homework is mine. I have written it on my own and in my own words. I have acknowledged in writing anyone with whom I have worked or from whom I have received help.
followed by your signature. Homework without this statement will not be accepted

 The last sentence of the statement requires you to list all the people who helped you with your work, for example: "I worked with Bob and Alice", "I recieved help from my tutor", "My mom helped me with problem 3".
