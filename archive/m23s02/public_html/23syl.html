April 1		 5.1, 5.2  Review series, Taylor series, and the radius of convergence. Series solutions to first order linear or second order constant coefficient. Solve  y''+y'=0 via series, and isolate fundamental solutions as S(x) and  C(x). Observe that sin(x) and cos(x) are also solutions. How are  S(x) and C(x) related to sin(x) and cos(x)? Leads to representation of functions by series.
