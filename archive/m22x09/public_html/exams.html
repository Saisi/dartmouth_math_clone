EXAM TWO: The second exam will be given on Monday (August 10th) during our regular class time.  The take-home portion will be due Wednesday (August 12th) at the beginning of class.  The exam will cover through and including Wednesday's lecture on August 5th.  It will emphasize material covered since the first exam.  This means sections 4.1 -- 4.7, the material on the matrix of a linear transformation covered in lecture based on part of section 5.4 and sections 3.1 and 3.2 on determinants.  Of course, material from the first part of the course, such as the Invertible Matrix Theorem, may be useful.

Unlike the first exam, the take home portion (as well as the in class portion) will be closed book and closed notes.  Also, no calculators or computing devices are allowed. 

Remember that your solutions to the take home portion are to be written on one side of 8.5" x 11" paper with each problem started on a new page.
EXAM ONE RESULTS: On the whole, the class did very well on a difficult exam.  My biggest critizism is that some of the work was sloppy.  Messy work is (somewhat) understandable if you are under time pressure, but there is no excuse on a take home exam where writing is a critical componet of the grade.  Just as in any writing assignment, I should not be seeing your first draft; I should be seeing the polished finished product.  I will be much less tolerant on future exams.  Also, you must learn to use mathematical terminology correctly.  That is part of learning the definitions.
