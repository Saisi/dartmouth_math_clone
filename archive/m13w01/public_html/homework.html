Homework Assignments

Homework for a given day is due at the next class meeting. This is the soft due-date. The hard due-date is posted on the problem set in WeBWorK. You may submit problems for credit up until the hard due-date, which will always be at 11:00 a.m. on the day of a class meeting. Answers will be be available at 1:00 p.m. the same day.

The homework problems listed daily in the form "Set w01dayXX" can be found by logging in to the WeBWorK Math 13 Problem Sets. You also may want to consult the page of student Frequently Asked Questions about WeBWorK, or the Introduction to WeBWorK.
