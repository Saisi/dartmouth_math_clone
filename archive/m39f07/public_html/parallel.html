Our handout on the parallel postulate and logic.

A few other statements equivalent to the Parallel Postulate:

  • The Pythagorean Theorem
  • The sum of the interior angles of a triangle is equal to the sum of two right angles
  • There exists a triangle whose interior angles sum to the sum of two right angles
  • Any three noncollinear points lie on a circle
  • If three angles of a quadrilateral are right angles, so is the fourth
