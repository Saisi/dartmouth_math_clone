Homework

  • The first homework assignment, due Thursday Sept 22, is to calculate the probability of getting exactly 4 different numbers when rolling 6 dice. For extra fun, try this (hard) problem: Prove that if you roll two sets of n n-sided dice, then there will be a non-empty subset of the red dice and a non-empty subset of the green dice with the same sum.
