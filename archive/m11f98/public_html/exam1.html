Disclaimer:

The practice exam offered below is the actual exam given in the Fall of 1997. There is absolutely no assertion being made here that this sample exam is representative of the problems to be found on this year's exam, but some overlap is certainly likely. We provide these problems to you solely to give you some indication of what kinds of problems one might expect.

Also note that there are some minor differences in the syllabus between last year and this year. In particular, we have not done the method of partial fractions, and we have not (yet) done any volume of revolution problems. On the other hand, we have done arclength. Eventually, solutions will be posted.
