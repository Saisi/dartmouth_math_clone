COURSE DESCRIPTION
(Excerpted from Course Descriptions on the Department of Mathematics web page.)

The three courses Mathematics 3, 8, and 13 provide a coherent three-term sequence in calculus. Mathematics 3 and 8 cover the basic calculus of functions of a single variable, as well as vector geometry and calculus of scalar-valued functions of several variables. Mathematics 13 covers the basic calculus of vector-valued functions of several variables.
