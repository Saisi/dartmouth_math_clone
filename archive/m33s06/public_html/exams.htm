Midterm: This will be a 2 hour, closed book exam. You are allowed a single-sided sheet of handwritten notes.

Take-home assignment: This will be a take-home, open book exam. Use of electronic resources such as MAPLE is encouraged, but students are not permitted to discuss the assignment with anyone other than the course instructor.

Final: The final will be a 3 hour, closed book exam. You are allowed a double-sided sheet of handwritten notes.
