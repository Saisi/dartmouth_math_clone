More on F.O.L.'s

Existence and Uniqueness Theorem for F.O.L.'s

Theorem: If the functions p and g are continuous on an open interval containing the point t = t0, then there exists a unique function that satisfies the differential equation

y' + p(t) y = g(t)

for each t in I, and that also satisfies the initial condition y(t0) = y0, where y0 is an arbitrary prescribed initial value.
