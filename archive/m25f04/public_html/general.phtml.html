Homework Policy

  • Homework will be assigned daily and will be due Wednesday of the following week.
  • Homework assignments will be announced in class and will be posted on the course assignment's web page.
  • Late homework will not be accepted in the absense of divine intervention or matters of similar weight.
  • Unexcused late and missing papers count zero.
  • Homework is to be written neatly using only one side of each page of paper. Do not use paper from a spiral notebook unless you can tear off the ragged edge. All pages of an assignment are to be stapled or paper-clipped together.
  • Write in complete sentences. You should be able to read your solutions as fluently as if you were reading your textbook.


Grades

The course grade will be based upon the scores of the homework, midterm exams and the final exam as follows:

Homework 100 points
Midterm Exams 200 points
Final Exam 150 points
Total 450 points


The Honor Principle

On Exams: No help given or received

On Homework: Students are permitted and encouraged to collaborate with one another on homework assignments. However, each student must complete the final draft of each assignment individually and independently. No copying!
