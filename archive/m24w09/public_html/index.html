Honors Linear Algebra

Instructor: Rustam Sadykov
Course: Honors Linear Algebra, M24


This course is an introduction to the fundamental concepts of linear algebra in abstract vector spaces. The topics and goals of the course are similar to those of Mathematics 22, but with an additinoal emphasis on mathematical abstraction and theory.
