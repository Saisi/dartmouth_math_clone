  • The Mersenne Primes
    Introduction to the history of the Mersenne primes; standard theorems and test; conjectures and lists.

    found in:  Science > Math > Number Theory > Prime Numbers  >  Mersenne
     
  • Woltman, George - GIMPS The Great Internet Mersenne Prime Search
    GIMPS is dedicated to a rigorous search for new Mersenne primes. Our goal is to test every Mersenne number with an exponent less than 20,500,000.
    http://www.mersenne.org/
    found in:  Science > Math  >  Mathematicians
