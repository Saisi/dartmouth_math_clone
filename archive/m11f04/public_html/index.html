This course provides an introduction to multivariable mathematics and calculus of vector-valued functions. Topics include coordinates and vectors in R2 and R3, differentiation and integration of parametrically defined functions with interpretations of velocity, acceleration, arclength and curvature. Other topics include iterated, double, triple and surface integrals including change of coordinates. The remainder of the course is devoted to vector fields, line integrals, Green's theorem, curl and divergence, and Stokes' theorem. Prerequisite: This course is for First-Year Students who have received two terms of AP credit based upon the BC exam. Dist: QDS.

Textbook: Calculus 5e, by James Stewart. The text is available at Wheelock Books.
