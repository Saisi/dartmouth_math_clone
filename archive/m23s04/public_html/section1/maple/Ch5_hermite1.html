Chapter 5. Series solutions of 2nd order ODEs

Hermite equation

> lambda:=1/2;

> deq:=diff(y(x),x,x)-2*x*diff(y(x),x)+lambda*y(x)=0;

> Order:=5;

> dsolve({deq,y(0)=a0,D(y)(0)=a1},y(x),series);

> sol:=convert(rhs(%),polynom);

> sol1:=(y0,y1)->subs({a0=y0,a1=y1},sol);

> plot(sol1(0,1),x=-2..2,y=-10..10);

> Order:=50;

> dsolve({deq,y(0)=a0,D(y)(0)=a1},y(x),series);

> sol:=convert(rhs(%),polynom):

> plot(subs({a0=0,a1=1},sol),x=-2..2,y=-10..10);

> plot(subs({a0=1,a1=0},sol),x=-2..2,y=-10..10);
