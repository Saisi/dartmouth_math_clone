Homework Assignments

Homework is due twice a week, on Mondays and Fridays by 8:00 AM. Homework assigned on Monday is due on Friday; homework assigned on Wednesday is due the following Monday; and homework assigned on Friday is due the next Friday. The homework problems listed daily in the form "Set m3f00dayXX" can be found logging in to the WeBWorK Math 3 Problem Sets. You also may want to consult M3 Introduction to WeBWorK, or the page of student Frequently Asked Questions about WeBWorK.
