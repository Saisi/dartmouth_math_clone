Math 43 Final Exam Discussion

What format should the final be?

Please express your opinion!

Ok, we have to have a final, but what form should it take.  Here are some possibilities.  Perhaps you can think of some others?  Anyway, I'd like to see what you prefer.  You can do so by going to the comments page and expressing your opinion.  You can also see everyone else's comments there and comment on the comments.  You can write anonymously if you prefer, and you can respond as often as you like.  I would like to hear at least once from everyone.

The Choices

  • We could have an in class final.  This would be the same length and format as the first two exams.
  • We could have an open book take home final.  The final would be available on the last day of classes (May 29) and due in my office at the end of our regularly scheduled exam: Saturday, June 1st at 11am.
  • We could have a closed book take home final.  This would be available via the web page on Friday and due in my office at 11:00am on Saturday.
  • We could have a combination of take home and in class final.  The take home portion would be closed book and due at the beginning of our exam period.  The in class portion would be as scheduled.  The take home portion could be either open or closed book.
