Tentative Syllabus

This course will (attempt to) cover the basics of class field theory for number fields.  As you might notice from the schedule of lectures below, stating and proving the main theorems of class field theory require a good deal of background.  As such, I'll be assuming a familiarity with the basic algebraic number theory covered in Chapter I and parts of Chapter III of the course text.  We'll develop the rest of the necessary background in the first half of the course.

Chapter Lectures Brief Description
II 4 Complete fields
III 1.5 Decomposition groups and the Artin map
IV 3.5 Analytic methods and ray classes
V 8 Class field theory
