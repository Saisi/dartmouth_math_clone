Homework Policy

Starting from the first week of  October, each  Monday at the end of class homework will be assigned. It will also be available here.  The homework  will be collected on Friday. Late submissions will not be accepted for any reason.  You may work together on these assignments, but you must write up your answers individually. Clearly indicate the exercise that you are solving by giving the section of the exercise and the exercise number. If you use more than one piece of paper, staple the pages together. Do NOT do your homework with anything except a pencil or a pen  (NO MARKERS, COLORED PENCILS, or CRAYONS). 

 

The total number of  points you earn on homework will be converted to a number out of 100 in computing final grades.
