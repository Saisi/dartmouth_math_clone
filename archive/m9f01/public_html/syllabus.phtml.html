Syllabus

The following is a tentative syllabus for the course. This page will be updated irregularly. On the other hand, the weekly syllabus contained in the Homework Assigments page will always be accurate.  You may notice that, currently, the syllabus is exactly the same as that of Math 8.  This is no coincidence - while the topics in Math 9 are virtually the same as those in Math 8, the treatment of these topics will differ.
