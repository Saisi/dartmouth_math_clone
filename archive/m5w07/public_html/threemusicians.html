"Three Musicians" by Pablo Picasso.

The picture was drawn in 1921, and now hangs in the Museum of Modern Art in New York.

(Picasso actually produced two paintings by the same name. The other hangs in the Museum of Art in Philadelphia.

Ref: Cooper, Philip. Cubism. London: Phaidon Press Limited, 1995; pg. 117.)

We consider the picture to be the central image that captures the spirit and intent of the course. In this context, what does the picture mean to you?
