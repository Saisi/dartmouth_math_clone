Homework

Homework will consist of a combination of problems on Webwork and written material from the textbook or similar to those in the textbook. The grading of the written homework will focus on showing your work clearly more than on getting the correct answer. Webwork problems will be assigned on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday to be due the midnight before the next of these days. Written work will be due on Tuesdays at the beginning of the x-period. Late homework will not be accepted.

Full solutions to many book problems are available at www.hotmath.com. Please email your instructor for password information. Note that most of the problems we assign will not have solutions available, but practice problems and problems similar to the homework will have solutions available.
