  2. Looking for a project topic? You might get some good ideas at The MacTutor History of Math Archive.
  3. There used to be a really great site name of Eric's Treasure Trove of Mathematics. You could search on any mathematical term and get a nice definition and good sources on it. But now it's being published (in book form) as the new CRC Concise Encyclopedia of Mathematics. As a result, due to copyright restrictions, each day you may only search for terms beginning with certain available letters. The available letters change daily. It's still a great site if the term you're looking for begins with an available letter; but, it's less than great if you're in a hurry and your letter is not available.
