Quizes: These will take place during x-hour. You will have the full 50 minutes. Use the notes, text and calculators is not permitted. You may however bring your integral tables.

Midterm: This is a 2 hour, closed book exam. You are allowed a single-sided sheet of handwritten notes and your integral tables. The use of calculators is not permitted.

Final: The final will be a 3 hour, closed book exam. You will be allowed a double-sided sheet of handwritten notes and your integral tables. The use of calculators is not permitted.
