Math 11 Exam Information


CALCULATOR POLICY: Calculators or computing devices will not be allowed on exams.   You may use calculators, Mathematica and/or Maple on homework, but it is your responsibility to be prepared to work the exams without the help of calculators or computers.  Naturally, the exams will be designed so that calculators are not required (or even helpful).
FINAL EXAM: The final exam covers the entire course: this includes sections 13.1 -- 13.5, 14.1 -- 14.4, 15.1 -- 15.8, 16.1 -- 16.8 and most importantly 17.1 -- 17.9.  Of course, you are not responsible with the bits of these sections we skipped in the reading assignments given on the homework page. (For example, we did not cover any of section 16.5.)  You should definitely expect the final to be heavily weighted towards the end of the course.  The final is three hours long as will be held in Filene Auditorium in Moore Hall from 3-6 on Saturday, December 6th.
