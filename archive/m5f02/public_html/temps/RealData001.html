> "Here we have maple read in our 100,000 actually randomly produced numbers. (Details: save it in Maple 7 with Netscape AS text, change name)":

M:=readdata(randomHT00,1):

>
"Here we take our list of number and make a program that
divides this list into m chunks of size n. For each of these chunks, we compute its associated z score and make a list of these z scores. Hopefully these z scores are normal!":


ViewData:=proc(n,m)
local i,T,H,j,sd;
H:=[];
for j from 0 to m-1
do
T:=0;
for i from 1 to n
do
T:=M[j*n+i]+T;
sd:=evalf((T-n*(1/2))/((1/2)*sqrt(n) ));
od;
H:=[op(H),sd];
od;
H;
end:

Nor:=plot((1/(sqrt(2*Pi)))*exp(-x^2/(2)),x =-3..3,color=blue):

>
"Here we view our z scores in order to compare them to the normal distribution. We used a trial with n=20 and m = 50":


P:=ViewData(20,50);
P1:=Histogram(P,5):
display(P1,Nor);

"Here I applied the simplest possible hypothesis test to the 100,000 term
list of heads and tails. Namely I computed the z value of the entire list.":

SimpHyp:=proc()
local i,T;
T:=0;
for i from 1 to nops(M)
do
T:=T+M[i];
od;
evalf((T-(1/2)*100000)/((1/2)*sqrt(100000)));
end:
