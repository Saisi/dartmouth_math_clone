COURSE DESCRIPTION

Mathematics 3 this term is a lecture course in introductory calculus that has been revised to include modeling in addition to the usual conceptual, analytical, and manipulative techniques of the subject. The course is taken by mostly first-year students, is taught in three sections in a lecture format, and meets three times per week.

We will be using the World Wide Web for tutorial videos, for calculus tools such as applets, and for WeBWorK, a homework-problem generator.

Calculus is truly a marvelous intellectual achievement, and we plan to have a lot of fun studying it.
