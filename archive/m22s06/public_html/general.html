Homework Policy

  • Homework will be assigned via the " Homework Assignments " link above.  Homework will be assigned for each lecture.  Although homework will be collected in class on Mondays, I hope that everyone will do the assignment for each lecture prior to the next lecture.  (I reserve the right to switch to collecting homework daily if the policy of collecting it weakly is abused.)  Late homework will not be accepted.   Our homework graders are Yiyi Ma and Henry Trier and you may email them directly with concerns about grading, etc.

The Honor Principle

On Exams: No help given or received

On Homework: Collaboration is permitted and encouraged -- a discussion of the general idea of the problem(s) with myself, the grader and others is desirable.  However, each student is expected to complete his or her assignments individually and independently. 

Disabilities

Students with disabilities are encouraged to contact me as soon as convenient to discuss appropriate accommodations. Also, they should stop by the Academic Skills Center in Collis Center to register for support services.

To: To: Religious Observances

I realize that some students may wish to take part in religious observances that fall during this academic term. Should you have a religious observance that conflicts with your participation in the course, please come speak with me before the end of the second week of the term to discuss appropriate accommodations.
