10.2 Fourier series

Example 1, page 551.

> Fourier := (x,n) -> 1-sum(cos((2*j-1)*Pi*x/2)/((2*j-1)^2),j=1..n)*8/(Pi^2);

> plot(Fourier(x,4),x=-4..4,y=0..2.5,color=blue);

> evalf(Fourier(1,8));

The Fourier polynomials converge pretty quickly in this case. Compare also with the next example.

This may get you an intuition for the Fourier Convergence Theorem discussed in Section 10.3.

Example 2, page 552.

> Fourier := (x,n) -> 1/3+sum(2*sin(j*Pi/3)*cos(j*Pi*x/3)/(j*Pi),j=1..n);

> plot(Fourier(x,50),x=-3..3,y=-.5..1.5,color=green);
