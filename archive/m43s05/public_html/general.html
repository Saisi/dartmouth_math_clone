Homework Policy

  • Homework will be assigned via the " Homework Assignments " link above.  Homework will be assigned for each lecture.  Although homework will be collected in class on Mondays, I hope that everyone will do the assignment for each lecture prior to the next lecture.  I reserve the right to switch to collecting homework daily if the policy of collecting it weakly is abused.   Our grader is Carlo Ordonez, and you may email him directly with concerns about grading, etc.

The Honor Principle

On Exams: No help given or received

On Homework: Collaboration is permitted and encouraged -- a discussion of the general idea of the problem(s) with myself, the grader and others is desirable.  However, each student is expected to complete his or her assignments individually and independently.
