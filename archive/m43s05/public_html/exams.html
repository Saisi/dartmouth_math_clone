A comment about grades.  In an advanced course like Math 43, the median grade is usually either a B+ or an A-.  Exactly where the median falls will vary from class to class depending on the absolute performance of the students.  I put grades on the exams to give students a general idea of where they stand.  A such grade should only be considered a rough estimate.

A comment about marking of exams.  I am always willing to discuss the grading of exams.  As a matter of good sense and courtesy, I expect you to have looked at the solutions provided above before you come to see me.  But. if you don't understand why points were taken off, it is important that you ask so that you can avoid losing points in the future.  It is even possible that the points may have been deducted in error.  It is more likely that I couldn't read or understand your reasoning -- or that you omitted your reasoning altogether.  I hold the take home work to a higher standard than the in class work.  But in either case, there is little excuse for incomplete sentences and/or work that is difficult to follow.
