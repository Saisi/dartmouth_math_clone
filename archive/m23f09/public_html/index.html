Differential Equations

Instructor: Erik van Erp
Course: Differential Equations, M23

A differential equation is an equation that defines a relationship between a function and its derivatives. Many scientific laws are formulated in terms of differential equations. In this introductory course you learn about basic types of differential equations and how to solve them. Topics include: first order differential equations, linear equations, series solutions, partial differential equations and Fourier series.
