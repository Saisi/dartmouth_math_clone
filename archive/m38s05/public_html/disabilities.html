Disabilities and Special Needs:

Students with disabilities who will be taking this course and may need disability-related classroom accommodations are encouraged to make an appointment to see their instructor as soon as possible, certainly within the first two weeks of the course. All conversations will remain confidential, although the academic skills center will be consulted to verify the documentation of the dissability. Also, students with disabilities should stop by the Academic Skills Center in Collis Center to register for support services. The coordinator Nancy Popian can be reached at 6-2014.
