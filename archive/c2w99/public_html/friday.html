FRIDAY CLASS DISCUSSIONS

The last class day of every week will be devoted to a class discussion of the issues that have been raised by the topics of the week. Normally, one of the two previous classes will have been led by Beatriz Pastor and the other by Dwight Lahr. The purpose of Friday's class is to bring the two together, to create a bridge between the two perspectives through a dialogue in which the class will participate.

Every Thursday we will post here the questions that will form the basis of the discussion the next day in class. You should review and think about these questions before class on Friday so that you are prepared to take part. You can suggest questions yourself by posting them before Thursday morning in the "POST A QUESTION" section of the website. We will review them as we are in the process of formulating a list to go here.
