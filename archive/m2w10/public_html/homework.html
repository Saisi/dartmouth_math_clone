Every week, there will be 3 homework assignments.

WebWork assignments will be due before the beginning of class on Wednesday and Friday.

Each Monday a written assignment will be distributed. These will be due by the beginning of class the following Monday.


Homework Policy:
  • Late homework is rarely accepted, and never without prior arrangement with the instructor. Starting assignments early will assure you have at least some work to submit for grading.

  • Homework is to be written neatly using 8 1/2 x 11 inch paper. Do not use paper from a spiral notebook unless you can tear off the ragged edge. All papers are to be stapled.

  • Use English. If you can't read your solutions aloud as fluently as if you were reading your textbook, try using nouns and verbs in your write ups!

  • Consult the honor principle as it applies to this course.
