About me

Welcome to my homepage. I am currently an Instructor in Applied and Computational Mathematics (IACM) in the Department of Mathematics at Dartmouth College and I look like picture on the right. Recently, I am working on new parallel fast integral equation solver for layered media using the Fast Multipole Method (FMM) (Please visit this website to see what is the FMM and who is working on) and treecodes. My publications and recent presentations are listed below. Please email me if there are any comments or questions.

Research

  • Wave propagation/scattering and its fast solver for layered media using the Fast Multipole Method and treecodes

  • Poisson-Boltzmann Equations for ion channels

  • Maxwell's equations for gratings

  • Photonics and Quantum mechanics applications (Photonic crystal, QD, Solar cells, etc)

  • My advisor's (Prof. Wei Cai) book on computational methods will be out sometime this year. Click the cover (I drew the graph) to see the table of contents, preface, and advance reviews
