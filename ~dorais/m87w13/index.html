François G. Dorais

Math 87 — Winter 2013

Categories, Topoi, and Logic

This is a reading course on the logical aspects of topos theory. The course will progress through basic category theory leading to topos theory and the internal logic of topoi.

The two primary reading sources for the course are:

  • Robert Goldblatt, Topoi: the categorial analysis of logic, Revised Edition, Dover, 2006.
    ISBN: 0-486-45026-0
  • Saunders Mac Lane, Categories for the Working Mathematician, Second Edition, Springer-Verlag, 1998.
    ISBN: 0-387-98403-8
Secondary reading sources are:
  • Joachim Lambek and Philip J. Scott, Introduction to Higher Order Categorical Logic, Cambridge University Press, 1986.
    ISBN: 0-521-24665-2
  • Peter T. Johnstone, Topos Theory, Academic Press, 1977.
    ISBN: 0-12-387850-0
  • Saunders Mac Lane and Ieke Moerdijk, Sheaves in Geometry and Logic: a first introduction to topos theory, Springer-Verlag, 1994.
    ISBN: 0-387-97710-4
