Music

In my spare time I take flute lessons with Alex Ogle. I am currently a member of the Dartmouth Symphony Orchestra. In 2011 I won the Dartmouth Culley Competition, and in 2013 I placed 1st in the woodwind division of the Culley competition

On Oct. 20, 2013 I gave a joint recital with Olivia Kang (mezzo soprano) and Scott Smedinghoff (piano).
