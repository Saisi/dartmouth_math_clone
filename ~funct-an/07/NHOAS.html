NEW HAMPSHIRE OPERATOR ALGEBRA SYMPOSIUM

November 15, 2007


The New Hampshire Operator Algebra Symposium, held this year at Dartmouth College is intended to develope a dialogue between the operator algebra groups at the University of New Hampshire and Dartmouth College. We hope this will be the first of many such meetings.
