The eighth annual East Coast Operator Algebras Symposium will take place at Dartmouth College, Hanover, New Hampshire, on October 23 and 24, 2010. The conference will start on Saturday morning and conclude in the early afternoon on Sunday.

The East Coast Operator Algebras Symposia have been established to promote the growth of operator algebras on the east coast and provide a forum for graduate students, postdoctoral scholars, and faculty to interact within the region.

Graduate students and postdoctoral fellows are strongly encouraged to attend. Support for travel and local expenses will be available. For details please see the support page.

The conference is funded in part by a grant from the National Science Foundation (NSF) as well as by Dartmouth College and the Edward Shapiro Fund at Dartmouth College.
