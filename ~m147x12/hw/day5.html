Group work on Math Camp 1 topic & organization

Form an overall plan for your math camp. Include goals (both general goals and specific mathematical goals), an outline of topics and timing (probably this will take the form of a draft spreadsheet), ideas about what activities might happen when, and a plan for sharing responsibility. Remember that there should always be at least one observer at any given time; there could be more, depending on your evaluation activities.
