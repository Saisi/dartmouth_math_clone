Homework due day 3

Designing an evaluation plan for one math camp goal

Each student takes one distinct math camp goal and designs an evaluation plan.

Please be detailed on how you will obtain numerical data to evaluate your assigned goal.

For instance if you plan to count number of comments/questions. What indicates success? A given number? A given rate? An increase over time? If you ask a question try to think of what answers are you looking and what will indicate success.
