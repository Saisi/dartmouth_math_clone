Take an online Implicit Association Test here. I recommend you do either the race one or one of the gender ones, although there are some other more obscure categories there too that you could try. It should take about 20 minutes, mostly binary categorization of words and faces. [The one I took had a second part with a bunch of questions about US politics, for the IAT people's own data gathering.
