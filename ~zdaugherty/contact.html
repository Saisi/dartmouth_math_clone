The Story:

I am currently a John Wesley Young Research Instructor at Dartmouth College. I did my graduate work at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, and my undergraduate degree at Harvey Mudd College. Before that, I grew up out west in Eugene, Oregon. My mathematical interests include representation theory, algebraic combinatorics, and Lie theory. When I'm not hard at work, I can usually be found out salsa or swing dancing, romping around in the woods, or listening to This American Life and sipping on good tea.
