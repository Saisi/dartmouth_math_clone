Syllabus

We plan to cover basic algebra presuming a basic knowledge of groups and rings from a standard undergraduate course in abstract algebra comparable to Math 71 here at Dartmouth. Topics should include
  • Review of basic linear algebra.
  • Theory of modules including free modules, localization, exactness, rank.
  • Application to canonical forms and diagonalizability.
  • Dual Modules.
  • Sequilinear forms.
  • Group actions and consequent group structure theorems.
  • Additional topics as time and interests permits.
