Projects:

    Shortly after the first mid-term, you will decide whether or not you want to do a project for this course. This is a     decision best made based on your goals for the course.


    Reasons you might want to do a project:

  1. 1.You want to learn more material than is being covered.

  2. 2.There is an application or result you want to learn more about that is not being covered.

  3. 3.A higher percentage of your final grade will be based on work from outside of class (i.e. not exams)


    Reasons you might not want to do a project:

  1. 1. The project is extra work.

  2. 2. There are no consequences for not doing a project.

How Projects Work:


    Once you have decided to do a project, there will be several check-ins and intermediate steps throughout the term so that any issues are headed off early. A complete description is available here.


    There is some flexibility in what a finished project will include. Components might include worked exercises, code, posters and expository material.
