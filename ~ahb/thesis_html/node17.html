Fokker-Planck equation for energy evolution

Stochastic evolution is generally governed by a Fokker-Planck equation, describing drift and diffusion of a density function (see Appendix A and [79]). The derivation of the correct Fokker-Planck equation for the time-evolution of the energy distribution is not trivial.
