Department Seminars



The definitive information resource for seminars is the daily events calendar.

Focused Research

Algebra and Number Theory - Topics in algebra and number theory (more of a reading group)

Applied & Computational Mathematics - Applied math, numerical analysis, scientific computation, physical and life sciences.

Combinatorics - Combinatorics and Applications.

Functional Analysis - Functional Analysis including Operator and C*-algebras.

Geometry and Toplogy - Geometry, Topology.

Logic - Logic, Set Theory, and Recursion Theory.

Number Theory - Elementary, analytic and algebraic number theory, modular forms, buildings.

Graduate Student

Graduate Student Seminar - Weekly series of talks by graduate students at all levels. No faculty in attendance.

Undergraduates

Undergraduate Activities - Dartmouth Mathematical Society, Association for Women in Mathematics, Undergraduate Talks, Movies, Thayer Prize and Putnam Exam training.
