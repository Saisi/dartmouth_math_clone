NHOTS

NHOTS

Saturday, April 10, 2010, 10am to 6pm

The New Hampshire Operator Theory Symposium is a biannual meeting of the functional analysts in and around New Hampshire. It is sponsored by Dartmouth College and the University of New Hampshire. Activities will include talks by graduate students and professors as well as lunch, dinner and drinks. This April NHOTS will be hosted at Dartmouth College in Kemeny Hall, room 108.
